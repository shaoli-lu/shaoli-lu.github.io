Welcome To My Site!

Richard Nixon once said: “The Chinese use two brush strokes to write the word crisis (危机). One brush stroke 危 stands for danger; the other 机 for opportunity. In a crisis, be aware of the danger - but recognize the opportunity”. This quote is apt for this COVID-19 pandemic. The virus is very contagious that is the danger 危. There is also the opportunity 机 side. As the virus keeps me in a shelter-in / stay-at-home state, I have time to learn and build web apps of my own! I've built a few. They are varied in functionalities and contents. I highlight a few on this page. There are apps I built for my developer needs and for my kids to preserve fond family memories. I tried to make all the apps mobile friendly. You can add the app on the home screen of your phone for instant access. Without further ado, let's dive in!
