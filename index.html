Welcome To My Site!

Richard Nixon once said: “The Chinese use two brush strokes to write the word crisis (危机). One brush stroke 危 stands for danger; the other 机 for opportunity. In a crisis, be aware of the danger - but recognize the opportunity.” This quote is apt for this COVID-19 pandemic. The virus is very contagious that is the danger 危 part. There is also the opportunity 机 part. As the virus keeps us in a shelter-in / stay-at-home state, we have more time for some "home" projects! For me, I have time to learn and build some web apps of my own that are ad-free. I've built a few. They are varied in functionalities and contents. I highlight a few on this page. There are apps I built for my developer needs and for my kids to preserve fond family memories. I tried to make the apps mobile friendly. To add web apps to the home screen for quick access, follow instruction here. Without further ado, let's dive in!

Memory Match


This is designed to test your memories. Click any cell to reveal a number. Click on another cell. Once a match is found the matched numbers will be visible. Find all the matches for the numbers in the shortest time. This game can be played using the number key as well!
