Memory Match


Memory match is a classic brain training game that helps improve memory and concentration skills. To play the game, you need to click or use the number key to reveal two cells at a time. If the numbers in the two cells match, they will remain visible. If they do not match, the cells will be hidden again. The goal of the game is to find all the matching pairs in the shortest amount of time.

Math Fun!

Math Fun is a game that helps improve your mental math skills by challenging you to solve algebraic equations quickly and accurately. To play the game, you will need to solve equations that use addition, subtraction, or multiplication, with numbers ranging from 0 to 20. The goal of the game is to get 10 correct answers without getting more than 2 incorrect answers.

Fun Math!

Fun Math is a game that helps improve your mental math skills by challenging you to solve simple algebraic equations quickly and accurately. To play the game, you will need to solve equations that use addition, subtraction, or multiplication, with numbers ranging from 0 to 10. The goal of the game is to get 10 correct answers without getting more than 2 incorrect answers.
